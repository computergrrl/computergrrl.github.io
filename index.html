About Me

I am a a book-loving, mom of 2 boys (one human, one feline). Originally from Arizona I replanted myself in Oregon, and have happily found my home in the Pacific Northwest.

In my most recent job role I worked with students who were learning software development at Treehouse. I love the problem-solving aspect of debugging, and of providing technical support. It's rewarding to help someone solve a problem, and I enjoy seeing people finally have that "aha" moment of understanding.

I like to watch shows or movies that can make me laugh, and I speak fluent gif and emoji.
